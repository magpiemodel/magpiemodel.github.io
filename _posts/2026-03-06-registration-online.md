The registration for the REMIND-MAgPIE26 workshop & stories event (**June 16<sup>th</sup> to June 19<sup>th</sup>**) is now [online](/workshops/remind-magpie26/home)! In case you want to join, please submit your registration as early as possible, as experience from our last event showed that available slots fill quickly. The event can be attended on-site at Potsdam Telegrafenberg or online, fees are 400€ for attending in person and 80€ online. There is also the possibility to only attend the first 3 days and focus on one of the models at a reduced 300€ fee. **Registration is required** and seats are limited.

Please click **[->here<-](/workshops/remind-magpie26/registration)** to open the external registration page.

The registration is handled through Eveeno, and you should receive an immediate confirmation via e-mail. Please contact us via <magpie@pik-potsdam.de> in case you do not receive a confirmation mail within a few days after registration.
